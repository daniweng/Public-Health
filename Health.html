Exploring a Decade of Environmental Health Challenges in Massachusetts

Welcome to a visual journey through a decade of environmental health data in Massachusetts, from 2011 to 2020. Our story unfolds through dynamic radial charts and informative pie charts, each painting a picture of the health challenges across the state.

A Tale Told Through Charts

  • Radial Charts: The Heartbeat of Data: Like the rings of a tree, each line in radial charts tells the story of a year - its struggles, its triumphs, and the health challenges faced by the people. Watch as the lines ebb and flow, depicting the rise and fall of hospitalization cases over the years.
  • Pie Charts: A Slice of Reality: Nestled within radial narrative, the pie charts offer a glimpse into the proportional impact of each health condition, year by year. They're like little windows, giving you a peek into the health landscape of Massachusetts in bite-sized portions.

A Note of Observation

My story also carries a whisper of caution: smaller characters like 'Heat Stress' and 'Carbon Monoxide Poisoning' might be harder to spot among the more dominant narratives. But remember, every line, every slice, is a crucial part of my story.

The Characters of Our Story

  1. Heat Stress: The silent scorcher, often overlooked but fiercely impactful in its wake, brings tales of endurance against sweltering heat.
  2. Carbon Monoxide Poisoning: The invisible threat, a silent intruder in homes, reminding us of the dangers we cannot see.
  3. Asthma: A familiar character in many stories, marking its presence with every breath, every wheeze, and every sigh.
  4. Myocardial Infarction (Heart Attack): The sudden twist in our tale, striking swiftly and demanding immediate attention and action.
  5. Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD): A lingering narrative of struggle and resilience, breathing life into the chronic battles many face.

The Foundation of the Tale

Rooted in the rich soil of data provided by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts , our story is as real as it gets. Every number, every chart, is a reflection of real lives, real challenges, and the enduring spirit of the people in Massachusetts.
